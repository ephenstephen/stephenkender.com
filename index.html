my typewriter addiction


Okay, long story short I helped a friend fix up an old typewriter they had laying around because I thought it would be a fun way to spend a lazy afternoon. And then I fell in love. And then a week later I bought my own (and now I'm realizing that I way overpaid for it, but it's whatever).

Since then (around September 2015), I've been able to curb my enthusiasm to a mere 4 more typewriters collected. One of them I got for free and never really use, and another I got just to tear apart and put back together (didn't get to the second part cause I broke it). But these seriously are some fantastic machines at the crossroads between mechanical awesomeness and sleek design; if you have one around, definitely try and use it! The tactile typing is so cool and it's really fun to type on one. If it's taking up space in your place, definitely let me know and I'd be happy to take it off your hands:)
