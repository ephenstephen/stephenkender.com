photography



I started getting into photography around 13, and learned a lot by practicing on my old point and shoot. It wasn't until a few years later that I splurged on a real DSLR for better pictures, but by then I had forgotten quite a bit. My weapon of choice is a Nikon D5300 with a 18-55mm kit lens.

I like the zoom lenses for a lot of wildlife photography, but nothing beats a wide angle landscape in my opinion. While I'm not going to toss up all my work on this site (bandwith and what not), I'll post my favorites in hopes that people enjoy them!

my typewriter addiction


Okay, long story short I helped a friend fix up an old typewriter they had laying around because I thought it would be a fun way to spend a lazy afternoon. And then I fell in love. And then a week later I bought my own (and now I'm realizing that I way overpaid for it, but it's whatever).

Since then (around September 2015), I've been able to curb my enthusiasm to a mere 4 more typewriters collected. One of them I got for free and never really use, and another I got just to tear apart and put back together (didn't get to the second part cause I broke it). But these seriously are some fantastic machines at the crossroads between mechanical awesomeness and sleek design; if you have one around, definitely try and use it! The tactile typing is so cool and it's really fun to type on one. If it's taking up space in your place, definitely let me know and I'd be happy to take it off your hands:)

plastics



I'm a plastics engineer out of the University of Massachusetts Lowell, which has a great program for plastics people. A great part of it is being able to utilize actual equipment and methods you'll use in industry, rather than just staying in class and learning the theory.

I really found my stride in design work, and I think I'd like to work in product design or design engineering, where you can take an idea and bring it to life (while still accounting for manufacturability, cost, etc.). Who knows where I'll be in 5 years, but it's great to know what you want to do and be!
